Welcome to Ghanshyam Nemiwal Technical Services LLC

25 Years Experience

Ghanshyam Nemiwal Technical Services LLC is a trusted name in tile and marble craftsmanship, delivering high-quality surface solutions that combine durability, elegance, and expert precision. Though the company was officially established 5 years ago, our foundation is built on over 25 years of specialized experience in the tile and marble industry.

With a strong focus on excellence and customer satisfaction, we offer professional installation, polishing, and surface finishing services for both residential and commercial projects. From luxurious marble floors to custom tile designs, we bring your vision to life — one tile at a time.

At Ghanshyam Nemiwal Technical Services LLC, we believe that great spaces begin with expert surfaces. Our commitment to quality, detail, and long-lasting results has earned us the trust of clients who seek nothing less than perfection.

Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

What services do you offer?
We specialize in professional tile and marble installation, floor and wall polishing, surface finishing, Screeding Work , chipping and repair services for both residential and commercial projects. Our expertise ensures precision, durability, and aesthetic appeal in every project.
How can I request a quote for my project?
Look for a "Contact" or "Get a Quote" page/link.
What types of tiles and marble do you work with?
We work with a wide range of materials including ceramic tiles, porcelain, vitrified tiles, and natural stones like marble, granite, and travertine. Whether it’s simple patterns or custom designs, we deliver excellence in every detail.
How much experience does your team have?
While Ghanshyam Nemiwal Technical Services LLC has been in operation for 5 years, our team brings over 25 years of hands-on experience in the tile and marble industry. This deep-rooted expertise ensures quality craftsmanship and reliable service.
Is Ghanshyam Nemiwal Technical Services LLC licensed and insured?
Yes, Ghanshyam Nemiwal Technical Services LLC is a fully licensed and insured company. We operate in compliance with all local regulations and industry standards, ensuring that our clients receive reliable, professional, and secure services. Our licensing and insurance provide peace of mind and protection for both residential and commercial projects.
How long does tile or marble installation take?
The duration depends on the area size, complexity of the design, and material type. However, we always strive to complete work on time without compromising quality. Timelines will be clearly communicated during the planning stage.
Are your services available for commercial spaces?
Yes, we handle residential, commercial, and industrial projects. From apartments and villas to offices, showrooms, and hotels — we’ve successfully delivered solutions across sectors.
Where are you based, and what areas do you serve?
We are based in the United Arab Emirates and proudly offer our services across all major Emirates, including Dubai, Sharjah, Abu Dhabi, Ras Al Khaimah, Sonapur, and Fujairah. Whether it's a residential or commercial project, we are available to serve clients throughout the UAE with quality and professionalism.

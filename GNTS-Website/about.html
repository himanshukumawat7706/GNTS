Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

What services does Ghanshyam Nemiwal Technical Services LLC offer?
We specialize in professional tile and marble installation, floor and wall polishing, surface finishing, Screeding Work , chipping and repair services for both residential and commercial projects. Our expertise ensures precision, durability, and aesthetic appeal in every project.
How can I request a quote for my project?
Look for a "Contact" or "Get a Quote" page/link.
What types of tiles and marble do you work with?
We work with a wide range of materials including ceramic tiles, porcelain, vitrified tiles, and natural stones like marble, granite, and travertine. Whether it’s simple patterns or custom designs, we deliver excellence in every detail.
How much experience does your team have?
While Ghanshyam Nemiwal Technical Services LLC has been in operation for 5 years, our team brings over 25 years of hands-on experience in the tile and marble industry. This deep-rooted expertise ensures quality craftsmanship and reliable service.
Is Ghanshyam Nemiwal Technical Services LLC licensed and insured?
Yes, Ghanshyam Nemiwal Technical Services LLC is a fully licensed and insured company. We operate in compliance with all local regulations and industry standards, ensuring that our clients receive reliable, professional, and secure services. Our licensing and insurance provide peace of mind and protection for both residential and commercial projects.
